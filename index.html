Nigeria's First Professor of Agrochemistry

A versatile chemist, Nigeria’s first Professor of Agrochemistry, Lami Nnamonu has a First-Class BSc degree in Chemistry Education; MSc in Organic Chemistry and did her PhD research in Durham University, UK on the Commonwealth Split-site Doctoral Scholarship leading to a PhD in Agrochemical Technology of the Federal University of Agriculture Makurdi – the first in sub-Saharan Africa. Her research in the field of agrochemistry is helping to actualize the core mandate of her university against the backdrop of current challenges of climate disadvantages and other factors generally affecting agricultural productivity. She was Head of the University Pre-Degree Department and the Acting Director of the Centre for Agrochemical Technology at the Federal University of Agriculture.

Professor Lami Nnamonu:

Making Science Fun and Wholesome

Professor Lami Nnamonu is a YouTuber who brings science and healthy living together in a fun and engaging way through her channel "Wholesome Lifestyle and Fun Chemistry."

Here's is what makes her content unique:

.Science with a Healthy Twist: Professor Nnamonu doesn't just teach chemistry concepts; she connects them to our well-being. She explains how the chemicals in different foods impact our bodies.

.Engaging Experiments: Forget boring lectures! Professor Nnamonu uses exciting experiments to demonstrate how chemical reactions play a role in health and well-being.

.Fun Learning: Her approach to chemistry is lighthearted and enjoyable, making it easier to grasp scientific concepts.

.Practical Applications: Professor Nnamonu goes beyond theory. She offers tips on incorporating chemistry principles into a healthy lifestyle. This could involve understanding how different foods react together during digestion or creating DIY natural cleaning products using safe chemical reactions..
